We are an innovative group focused on developing a new generation of entrepreneurs through technology, our history starts from Future Skills Bootcamp, a coding bootcamp which was organized by Ministry of ICT and Innovetion (MINICT) and Rwanda Coding Academy (RCA),with a scope of spreading coding skills in young people from different regions of the counrty. We saw that the skills we obtained from this Bootcamp wasn't meant to be kept but to be used in solving different problems in our country.

As we are innovative, we saw this as an opportunity to start Ishya Solutions to turn our ideas into actions and also help other innovative young people to work with us by developing their projects and using their skills to contribute to the development of our country.

Mission & Vision

We are an innovative group focused on developing more digital solutions, which will minimize different problems that the world is currently facing; through, helping different young people to develop their projects, promoting the practice of entrepreneurship skills, and helping different institutions to get digital platforms.

Ishya Solutions is willing to see a bright future of the world rooted on digital solutions and also low levels of unemployment in youths, by helping each other we will be able to develop together.
